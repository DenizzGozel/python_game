Welcome to the Overflow Board Game

Game Objective

Control the entire board by placing gems and causing them to overflow into neighboring cells. If you manage to turn every cell on the board into your color, you win!

How to Play

  1. Placement: On your turn, place a gem on an empty cell or one that already belongs to you.
  2. Overflow Mechanic: If the number of gems in a cell reaches the number of the direct neighboors that cell has, the cell overflows and the gems spread to adjacent cells, flipping their ownership to your color.
  3. AI Opponent: Our built-in AI will automatically place gems for the opposing player. Keep an eye on its moves!
  4. Winning Condition: The game ends when the entire board is controlled by a single color.
